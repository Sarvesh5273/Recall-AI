## Full Description (4000 chars max)

**English:**
Recall AI turns your handwritten kirana store ledger into a digital inventory in seconds.

📸 Just take a photo of your ledger page
🤖 AI reads Hindi, Gujarati, Marathi & English handwriting
📦 Automatically matches items to your inventory
📱 Works offline — syncs when you're back online
🔒 Secure PIN lock for your data

**How it works:**
1. Open the app and tap the camera
2. Take a photo of your ledger page (stock-in or stock-out)
3. AI extracts item names and quantities
4. Your inventory updates automatically
5. Items AI can't recognize go to your Inbox for quick review

**Why Recall AI?**
- No typing needed — just scan and go
- Understands regional languages and handwriting
- Works even without internet (syncs later)
- Free plan: 60 scans/month
- Basic plan: 300 scans/month (₹99)
- Pro plan: Unlimited scans (₹299)

Built for Indian kirana store owners who want to go digital without changing how they work.
